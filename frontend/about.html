Company History:

Peak Power was founded in 2010 in Kharkiv, Ukraine, by a group of enthusiasts who share a passion for a healthy lifestyle and sports. Our company emerged from the belief that high-quality sports nutrition and equipment should be accessible to everyone striving for better physical fitness.

Mission and Values of Peak Power:

The mission of Peak Power is to provide high-quality products and equipment that contribute to unlocking the full potential of each individual on their fitness journey. We value:

  • Quality and safety of our products.
  • Continuous improvement.
  • Support for an active and healthy lifestyle.
